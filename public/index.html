ABOUT ME

I am a high school student passionate about mathematics, artificial intelligence, robotics, and scientific research. My journey began with robotics, where I discovered how software, engineering, and teamwork come together to solve real-world problems. Since then, I have expanded my interests into artificial intelligence, computational science, and mathematical thinking, always looking for opportunities to learn beyond the classroom. I enjoy building projects that combine technical depth with practical impact, whether developing robot software, exploring AI-driven solutions, or participating in research and engineering competitions. Beyond technical work, I value leadership, collaboration, and continuous learning, and I strive to create meaningful solutions while helping others grow alongside me.
